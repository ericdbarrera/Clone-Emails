The Last Bookstore has started a new membership program! We currently have two membership levels available. Each membership is on a yearly basis and includes a store-wide discount on purchases at any of our bookstores, whether it's The Last Bookstore, Lost Books (Montrose), or Skull-Face (Honolulu). (Can you believe we have 3 unique stores now?!).

The Portal membership is $25 per year and includes a 15% discount, while the Tunnel membership is $50 per year with a 20% discount. Both come with TLB accessories like stickers and pens. As we expand the program later this year, higher level members will receive added benefits like free merch items and book bundles, early access shopping, curated lists of new releases, and more!

Want to become part of our Last Bookstore family and help keep us around? You can sign up to become a member online here or at checkout on your next in-person visit to any of our stores. We hope to see you browsing our shelves very soon!
